History

Mountain Name Rafting began in 1978 as the dream of two lifelong friends, Sarah Collins and Jack Rivers, who shared a love for exploring the untamed waters of the Rockies. Inspired by the rugged beauty of Mount Cascade, the company's namesake, they transformed their passion into a business dedicated to offering thrilling white water adventures. What started as a small operation with a single raft has grown into one of the region's premier outdoor recreation companies. From the roaring rapids of Devil's Canyon to the serene bends of Whispering Stream, Mountain Name Rafting has guided adventurers through some of nature's most breathtaking landscapes for over four decades.

Committed to preserving the natural beauty that defines their excursions, Mountain Name Rafting has also become a leader in environmental stewardship. In the early 1990s, the company launched the "Clear Waters Initiative," which focused on cleaning up local waterways and supporting sustainable tourism practices. Their innovative blend of adventure and conservation has earned them accolades and a devoted following among outdoor enthusiasts. Whether you're a seasoned rafter or a first-timer, Mountain Name Rafting's experienced guides and rich history make every trip unforgettable.
